Works

Elos is a rapping duo of two cousins. At the fall of 2017 they asked me to produce their first ever musicvideo. THe work was alot of directing the artists on how to act in a musicvideo. With good collaboration and only a few different lenses I managed to capture a good essence of the vibe these two guys gave off. The video ended up being their most liked piece. The whole project was done by myself solo from the start to the finished version.





Our snowboarding group of friends got together and threw tricks for this montage just for fun. It showcases our regular riding by having fun and killing it on the slopes. The filming was additionally done by Jerry Lahtinen who had been filming our buddies for a couple months in Talma. I Took my camera to the slopes. captured the rest of the material and then edited it by myself.
